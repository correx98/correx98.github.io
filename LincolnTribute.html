Abraham Lincoln

One of the truly great men of all time

Lincoln in 1863 at the age of 54

Here's a time line of Abraham Lincoln life:

  • 1809 February 12: Abraham Lincoln is born in Nolin Creek in Kentucky. His humble home was a one room log cabin
  • 1811: The Lincoln family move to a 230 acre farm near Sinking Spring
  • 1816 December: The Lincoln family move to Indiana
  • 1818 October 5: The death of his mother, Nancy Hanks Lincoln
  • 1819 December 2: Thomas Lincoln marries a widow, Sarah Bush Johnston who becomes stepmother to Abraham Lincoln
  • 1830 March: The Lincoln family move to Illinois. First political speech of Abe in favor of improving navigation on the Sangamon River
  • 1831: Abe settles in New Salem, Illinois working as a clerk in the village store
  • 1832 March: Abe becomes a candidate for Illinois General Assembly
  • 1834 August 4: Lincoln is elected to the Illinois General Assembly. Abe starts to study law
  • 1835:Becomes leader of the Whig Party
  • 1836 August 1: Re-elected to the Illinois General Assembly. Lincoln receives his law license
  • 1837 April: Moves Springfield. Establishes as a law partner of John T. Stuart
  • 1839 December 3: Admission to United States Circuit Court
  • 1842 November 4: Abe marries Mary Todd in Springfield
  • 1844 December: Abe sets up his own law practice and dissolves partnership with Logan
  • 1846 May 1: Nominated as Whig candidate for U.S. Congress
  • 1846 August 3: Abe is elected to the U.S. House of Representatives
  • 1860 November 6: Abraham Lincoln is elected as 16th U.S. president
  • 1865 April 9: General Robert E. Lee surrenders his Confederate Army to General Ulysses S. Grant at the village of Appomattox Court House in Virginia
  • 1865 April 14: President Lincoln and his wife Mary go to Ford's Theater. During the third act of the play John Wilkes Booth shoots the president in the head
  • 1865 April 15: President Abraham Lincoln dies at 7:22 in the morning. Vice President Andrew Johnson assumes the presidency
  • 1865 May 4: Abraham Lincoln is buried in Oak Ridge Cemetery, outside Springfield, Illinois

"And in the end it is not the years in your life that count, it's the life in your years." Abraham Lincoln
